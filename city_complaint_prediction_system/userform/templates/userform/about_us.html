About Us

Learn more about our Smart City Complaint Prioritization & Resolution Prediction System.

Project Introduction

The Smart City Complaint Prioritization & Resolution Prediction System is a web-based platform designed to help citizens report city-related issues easily. The system uses machine learning to analyze complaints and predict both their priority level and estimated resolution time, helping city authorities respond faster.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve communication between citizens and municipal authorities by providing a transparent and efficient complaint management system. We aim to ensure that critical issues receive prompt attention and that city services operate effectively.

What Our System Does

  • Allows citizens to register complaints online
  • Categorizes complaints based on issue type and severity
  • Predicts complaint priority using machine learning
  • Estimates resolution time for reported issues
  • Helps authorities focus on the most impactful problems

How the System Works

Citizens submit complaints online. The system analyzes details like category, area, severity, and affected people. Using machine learning, it predicts complaint priority and estimates the expected resolution time, helping authorities address the most urgent issues.

Future Improvements

We plan to enhance our system by introducing mobile app support, real-time notifications, mapping complaints on a city map using GIS, and further integration with municipal service systems.
